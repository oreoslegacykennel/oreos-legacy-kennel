About Oreo

Small, rare, and built for legacy.

Oreo’s coat reads deep black with warm brown undertones in sunlight and refined white accents. He’s healthy, well-kept, and offered for ethical, intentional breeding only.

Temperament & Presence

Calm confidence, strong eye contact, and “main-character energy.” Oreo photographs well, adapts quickly, and stays composed — great for breeding programs that value both health and type.

Service Area

Based in Conyers, Georgia. Nationwide service supported through shipped semen with a licensed reproduction veterinarian. Local options can be discussed with approved breeders.

Stud Services

Simple pricing. Clear terms. Protected health.

You’re choosing between a flat fee or a fee + puppy arrangement. A standard stud agreement is required before any breeding or records exchange.

Option A

$2,100 flat fee

  • Best for breeders who don’t want a litter pick arrangement
  • Shipped semen coordinated through licensed reproduction veterinarian
  • Includes one free repeat (see policy below)
Option B

$1,800 + pick of the litter

  • Lower upfront cost + quality pick arrangement
  • Pick terms (timing, selection method) defined in agreement
  • Shipped semen coordinated through licensed reproduction veterinarian

Repeat Breeding Policy

One free repeat is offered if no pregnancy is confirmed within 12 months, provided all agreement terms were followed and veterinary timing/handling protocols were met.

Preferred Method

Shipped semen (chilled/frozen) is the preferred option for safety, scheduling, and health control. Local options can be considered case-by-case after screening.
